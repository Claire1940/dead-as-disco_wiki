## Combat Mechanics: The Art of the Swing

Success in the **dead as disco epic games** ecosystem depends on your ability to maintain a combo. The "swing" mechanic is not just a simple attack; it is a momentum-based move that gains power based on your previous actions. If you successfully "pack the house" by landing consecutive hits without taking damage, your character enters a Flow State.

In this state, your movement speed increases, and your "Tech-Ret" abilities cool down faster. However, The Prophet and other high-level bosses have "Choke" mechanics designed to break your flow. These are unpredictable, off-beat attacks that require visual cues rather than just auditory ones.

### Essential Combat Moves

1.  **The Basic Swing:** Your bread-and-butter attack. Best used in a 4-beat pattern.
2.  **The Juggle:** A vertical launcher that puts enemies into a vulnerable state.
3.  **The Tech-Dash:** A short-range teleport that provides frames of invincibility.
4.  **The Monument Smash:** A heavy overhead hit that can break through boss shields.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not rely solely on the beat. Late-game bosses like The Prophet will intentionally skip beats or change the tempo to throw off your timing.
