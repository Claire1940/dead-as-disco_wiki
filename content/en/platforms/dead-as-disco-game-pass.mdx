## Mastering the Rhythm-Based Combat System

The heart of Dead As Disco lies in its combat, which takes the free-flow mechanics popularized by the *Batman: Arkham* series and syncs them to a persistent musical beat. Every punch, kick, and counter-move must be performed in time with the track to maximize damage and maintain your flow.

### Core Combat Actions

To succeed, you must master the "Beat Marker," an optional visual aid that helps players stay in sync with the music. Success is measured by your "Fever Meter," which builds as you land successful hits and dodges without breaking your streak.

| Action | Input Timing | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | On the Beat | Deals standard damage and builds Fever. |
| **Counter** | On the Beat (Flash) | Reverses enemy attacks and maintains flow. |
| **Dodge** | On the Beat | Avoids unblockable attacks and re-positions Charlie. |
| **Fever Takedown** | Full Meter | Instant, cinematic finisher on standard enemies. |

### Advanced Specialist Enemies

As you progress through the neon-soaked streets, you will encounter more than just standard hired goons. The game introduces specialist types that require specific rhythmic strategies to defeat.

1.  **Robot Ladies:** These enemies fire long-range laser beams. You must dodge the initial charge-up and counter the final pulse on the fourth beat.
2.  **Riot Shield Heavies:** These foes cannot be attacked from the front. Players must use a rhythmic "vault" move to get behind them and strike their exposed back.
3.  **Sonic Snipers:** These enemies disrupt your rhythm by firing off-beat projectiles. Prioritize these targets to keep your combo intact.
